# Traditional Archetype System

## Purpose

This module is NOT used for fortune telling.

This module is NOT used for future prediction.

This module is ONLY used to enrich personality modeling and narrative diversity.

The goal is to provide additional cultural archetypes that help generate more immersive life trajectories.

---

# Design Principle

Parallel Life does not predict destiny.

Parallel Life explores possible life paths.

Traditional cultural elements are treated as:

* Personality archetypes
* Narrative inspirations
* Decision-making tendencies

NOT as deterministic outcomes.

---

# Input Sources

The system may optionally reference:

* Birth date
* Birth time
* Birth location
* Self-assessment questionnaire
* User decisions during gameplay

These inputs contribute to personality modeling only.

---

# Five Archetype Framework

Instead of using fortune-telling language, use five behavioral archetypes.

## Wood Archetype

Characteristics:

* Growth-oriented
* Curious
* Exploratory
* Innovative

Potential tendencies:

* Entrepreneurship
* Creativity
* Learning

Narrative style:

Often attracted by new opportunities and unknown paths.

---

## Fire Archetype

Characteristics:

* Expressive
* Passionate
* Energetic
* Influential

Potential tendencies:

* Leadership
* Public communication
* Social networking

Narrative style:

Often driven by excitement and personal vision.

---

## Earth Archetype

Characteristics:

* Stable
* Responsible
* Practical
* Reliable

Potential tendencies:

* Long-term planning
* Family focus
* Organizational roles

Narrative style:

Often values security and gradual progress.

---

## Metal Archetype

Characteristics:

* Disciplined
* Analytical
* Strategic
* Independent

Potential tendencies:

* Management
* Finance
* Decision-making

Narrative style:

Often seeks efficiency and structure.

---

## Water Archetype

Characteristics:

* Reflective
* Adaptive
* Observant
* Flexible

Potential tendencies:

* Research
* Design
* Deep thinking

Narrative style:

Often succeeds through adaptation rather than force.

---

# Personality Synthesis

The final personality model should combine:

40% Self-assessment

30% Behavioral decisions

20% Life experience

10% Traditional archetype

Traditional archetypes must never dominate the model.

---

# Narrative Rules

Allowed:

"You tend to explore unfamiliar opportunities."

"You appear comfortable adapting to change."

"You often value stability before taking risks."

Not Allowed:

"You are destined to become wealthy."

"You will definitely succeed."

"You are fated to marry late."

"You will experience a major crisis at age 35."

---

# Compliance Rules

The system must never:

* Predict death
* Predict illness
* Predict marriage outcomes
* Predict wealth outcomes
* Predict specific future events

The system may only discuss:

* Tendencies
* Preferences
* Behavioral patterns
* Alternative possibilities

---

# Product Principle

Parallel Life explores possibilities.

It does not determine destiny.
